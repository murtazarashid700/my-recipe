JELLY:

IS ONE OF THE EASIEST AND FUN MAKING ITEM IN FOODS.

JELLY CAN BE MADE IN ANY SHAPE AS WELL AS IN ANY COLOUR .


THERE ARE MANY FLOVERS IN JELLY LIKE

  • MANGO
  • BANANA
  • STRAWBERRY
  • ETC

JELLY INGREDIENTS:

  1. SUGAR
  2. ADIPIC ACID
  3. EDIBLE GUMS
  4. POTASSIUM CITRATE
  5. ARTIFICIAL FLAVOUR
  6. POTASSIUM CHLORIDE
  7. PERMITTED FOOD COLOUR

MAKING METHOD:

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in saucepan.
  2. Empty all the contents of the packets into boiling water.
  3. stir till dissolved.
  4. Pour the mixture into a mould and allow to cool at room temperature.
  5. put it in refrigerator for setting.
  6. Now seperate the jelly from sides of mould using knife.
  7. Gently shake and invert mould carefully over serving dish.
  8. enjoy delicious jelly
